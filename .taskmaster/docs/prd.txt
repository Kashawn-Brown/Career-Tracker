Overview
Task-Master is a full-stack job application tracker designed to replace traditional spreadsheets and help users manage and analyze their job search. It provides a structured, editable, and expandable table view where users can log job applications, add details (like documents, deadlines, and notes), and track progress. It is valuable for job seekers who want to stay organized, take actionable steps (e.g., follow-ups), and eventually view meaningful job search statistics.
